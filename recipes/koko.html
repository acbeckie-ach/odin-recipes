Hausa Koko

Description

Hausa koko in Ghana also known as millet porridge is a popular
Ghanaian breakfast delicacy often taken with ‘bofrot’ or bread.
If you want to learn how to prepare this awesome breakfast with
ease, then wait, don't go anywhere yet.
Just read this recipe to the end.

Ingredients

  • Millet Flour
  • Cloves
  • Ginger
  • Milk
  • Suagr
  • Salt
